--- layout: article title: What we prize date: '2013-10-06T11:23:00.000-04:00' author: JP Bohanan tags: - reflections modified_time: '2013-10-06T11:23:00.454-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2749744699161548108.post-2280137867789964251 blogger_orig_url: http://christonthemountaintop.blogspot.com/2013/10/what-we-prize.html --- In my prayers on Wednesday, I read Psalm 12 and was struck by the end of verse 8:
That which is worthless is highly prized by everyone.


How often are my priorities shaped by "everyone" rather than by the love of God? I want to renew my focus on what matters most.

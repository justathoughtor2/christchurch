With no sign of a pending peace agreement, thousands of Syrian-Armenian refugees have traveled to Armenia in search of a secure and safe refuge. More than 10,000 Syrian-Armenian refugees have fled to Armenia since the fighting started two years ago; among them, 7,000 have applied for residency.

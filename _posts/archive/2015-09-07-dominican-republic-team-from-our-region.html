Our team had two nurses, including my daughter Christina. We asked if Mission Central had items in their warehouse that could be used for such a project”, said Rick. The answer was an overwhelming YES! Jean Norris, a nurse volunteer at Mission Central and Ray Harden, Mission Central Warehouse Operations Manager, gathered bandages, gauze, creams, syringes, sponges, cotton balls and even some small medical equipment for us to use in Cristobal, DR.
